If you're a professional in the DC metro area considering an EB-2 National Interest Waiver, you don't necessarily need an employer. You need a strong, well-documented case. Capitol Law Partners works with NIW self-petitioners across Northern Virginia, DC, and Maryland to build petitions that satisfy the three-prong Dhanasar test. Here's how we help.

## Who we work with
Researchers, engineers, entrepreneurs, healthcare professionals, and other advanced-degree or exceptional-ability individuals whose work has national importance. Many of our NIW clients are based in the DC metro's research, tech, and policy communities.

## Building the Dhanasar case
We help you frame your endeavor's national importance, document why you're well positioned to advance it, and make the case that waiving the job offer benefits the U.S. The work is in turning your record into evidence that maps to each prong.

## Evidence and strategy
From assembling independent expert letters to organizing citations, press, funding, and results, we focus the petition on what USCIS actually weighs. We'll also tell you honestly if the NIW isn't your strongest route.

## Local and accessible
Being in the DC metro means we understand the institutions, employers, and fields our clients work in. Whether you're in Virginia, DC, or Maryland, we make the self-petition process manageable.
