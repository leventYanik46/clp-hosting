Virginia's tech sector — concentrated in Northern Virginia but growing statewide — runs on specialized talent, and a lot of that talent needs work authorization. Capitol Law Partners provides immigration counsel built for how tech companies actually hire, from fast-moving startups to established firms. Here's where we help.

## Hiring engineers and specialists
We match roles to the right visa: H-1B for specialty-occupation hires, O-1 for standout engineers and researchers, L-1 for transfers from overseas offices, and E-2 or other routes for founders. The goal is getting talent onboard without timeline surprises.

## Founder and startup immigration
For founders, we navigate O-1 self-sponsorship structures, E-2 investor options, and the path toward EB-1A or NIW green cards. Startups have unique constraints, and we plan around them.

## Retaining talent long-term
Hiring is only half the problem; keeping people is the other half. We build green card strategies — EB-1, EB-2 NIW, PERM-based EB-2/EB-3 — so your best engineers can stay permanently.

## Scaling compliance
As you sponsor more workers, compliance gets heavier. We help Virginia tech employers put repeatable, audit-ready processes in place so immigration scales with headcount rather than becoming a bottleneck.
