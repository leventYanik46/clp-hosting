Washington, DC employers competing for specialized talent often need to sponsor H-1B workers, and the cap, lottery, and compliance rules make getting it right essential. Capitol Law Partners helps DC-area employers manage H-1B sponsorship from registration through filing. Here's how we support you.

## Lottery registration and strategy
We help you identify candidates early, prepare the documentation before the spring registration window, and register correctly. Because selection isn't guaranteed, we also plan backup options for candidates who aren't picked.

## Building the petition
From the Labor Condition Application to the specialty-occupation argument and wage compliance, we assemble petitions designed to withstand scrutiny, and we respond to requests for evidence when USCIS asks for more.

## Compliance for DC employers
H-1B sponsorship creates ongoing obligations, including public access files and maintaining the petition's terms. We help DC-area employers stay compliant and ready for review.

## Beyond the H-1B
When the lottery doesn't work out or another category fits better, we evaluate L-1, O-1, E-2, and green card routes. We work with startups, established firms, and organizations across the DC area to keep hiring on track.
