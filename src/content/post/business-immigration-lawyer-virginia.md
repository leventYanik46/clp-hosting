Virginia companies — from Northern Virginia tech firms to growing businesses across the state — increasingly need to hire and retain foreign talent. Capitol Law Partners helps Virginia employers navigate work visas and employment-based green cards so a key hire doesn't stall over immigration. Here's how we support employers in the Commonwealth.

## Work visas for Virginia employers
We handle the full range of employment visas — H-1B specialty occupation petitions, L-1 intracompany transfers, O-1 extraordinary ability cases, E-2 treaty investor matters, and TN filings — matching the right category to each role and managing the process end to end.

## Green card sponsorship
For employers ready to make a hire permanent, we manage PERM labor certification, I-140 petitions, and the EB-1, EB-2, and EB-3 categories, including National Interest Waivers for qualifying professionals.

## Compliance built for VA businesses
Sponsoring foreign workers comes with ongoing obligations. We help Virginia employers set up the records and processes that keep them audit-ready, so growth doesn't create exposure.

## Why local matters
Working with counsel familiar with the Northern Virginia and DC-metro talent market means practical advice tailored to how local companies actually hire. We work with founders, HR teams, and established employers across Virginia.
