Join Capitol Law Partners for a free, practical webinar on getting ready for 2026 H-1B cap season. If your company plans to sponsor H-1B workers, this session walks employers through the registration lottery, the key deadlines, and how to position candidates before the window opens — so you're prepared rather than scrambling.

## What you'll learn
How the H-1B registration lottery works, what to prepare before the spring window, how to document specialty occupations and wages, and what to do if a candidate isn't selected. We'll cover the timeline from registration through petition filing.

## Who should attend
HR leaders, founders, hiring managers, and operations staff at companies that plan to sponsor H-1B workers in the 2026 cycle — whether you're filing your first petition or your fiftieth.

## Format
A live presentation followed by a Q&A where you can ask about your company's specific situation. Practical and employer-focused, not a sales pitch. We'll share takeaways you can act on right away.
